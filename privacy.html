Idle Monster Farm

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 3, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Idle Monster Farm handles information. Idle Monster Farm is a free-to-play idle merge game developed by Mamduh. The game does not require account registration.

Information We Collect

The developer does not operate a custom backend, account system, or cloud save service for this app. The game does not include login, multiplayer, user-generated content, or a backend account.

Because the game uses Google AdMob rewarded ads, advertising partners may collect or process device identifiers, ad interaction data, diagnostics, or similar data as described in their own policies.

Local Save Data

Idle Monster Farm stores gameplay progress locally on your device. This local save data may include game progress, resources, upgrades, settings, and similar gameplay state.

The developer does not operate a custom backend or cloud save service for this app. If the app is uninstalled, the local save data may be deleted by the device or operating system.

Advertising

The game uses Google AdMob to show optional rewarded ads. Rewarded ads are used so players can choose to watch an ad in exchange for an in-game reward.

AdMob and Google may collect or process device and advertising-related information according to Google's own policies and settings.

Third-Party Services

Idle Monster Farm uses Google AdMob for advertising. Third-party services may process information under their own privacy policies, terms, and user controls.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Children's Privacy

Idle Monster Farm does not require users to create an account or submit personal information to the developer. The app is not described here as being made specifically for children.

If you believe a child has provided personal information directly to the developer, please use the contact information below.

Data Security

Gameplay progress is stored locally on the device. No method of storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure. Users should keep their device and operating system updated.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated when the app changes or when legal, platform, or advertising requirements change. The "Last updated" date will be changed when this page is updated.
