Rewarded Ads

Idle Monster Farm uses Google AdMob to show optional rewarded ads. If a rewarded ad does not load, check your internet connection and try again later. Ad availability can depend on Google AdMob, device settings, region, network conditions, and ad inventory.

If an ad finishes but the reward is not granted, return to the game screen and wait a moment before trying again.

Save Data

Idle Monster Farm saves gameplay progress locally on your device. There is no login, backend account, or cloud save service for this app.

Local save data may include game progress, resources, upgrades, settings, and similar gameplay state.

Reinstall Behavior

Because progress is saved locally, uninstalling or clearing app data may delete your save data. Reinstalling the app may start a new game if the device or operating system removed the local save.

Basic Troubleshooting

  • Restart the app if gameplay or ads stop responding.
  • Check that your device has a working internet connection for ads.
  • Update the app when a newer version is available.
  • Keep your device operating system updated.
